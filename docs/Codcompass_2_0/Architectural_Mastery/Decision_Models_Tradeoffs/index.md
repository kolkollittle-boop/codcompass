# Decision Models & Trade-offs

## Core Theory & Mental Models

- **Option framing**: architecture is choosing among constrained futures—document alternatives discarded, not only the winner.
- **Reversibility gradient**: some decisions are one-way doors; identify them early and escalate decision rights appropriately.
- **Coupling budgets**: integrate where volatility aligns; isolate where change rates diverge.
- **Temporal trade-offs**: optimize for time-to-learn early; for scale economics and predictability later—same codebase rarely optimizes both simultaneously.

## Learning Path & Maturity Stages

1. **Explicit decision hygiene** — Problem statements, constraints, and success metrics before solution browsing.
2. **Trade-off catalogs** — Recurring patterns (consistency vs. availability, build vs. buy) with org-specific weighting.
3. **ADR practice** — Lightweight records that age well; link to superseding decisions without orphaning history.
4. **Executive synthesis** — Translate engineering trade-offs into risk, cost, and time narratives for non-implementers.
