# Systematic Learning Architect

## Core Theory & Mental Models

- **Learning is a system property**: individuals learn; organizations only learn when mechanisms capture and propagate insight.
- **Double-loop learning**: fixing incidents (single loop) vs. questioning assumptions that caused them (double loop).
- **Curriculum thinking**: capability gaps map to staged outcomes—not one-size certification theater.
- **Anti-pattern**: hero-dependent mastery without playbooks—creates bottlenecks and fragility.

## Learning Path & Maturity Stages

1. **Competency mapping** — Roles, desired skills, and evidence of proficiency tied to business outcomes.
2. **Guided pathways** — Sequences blending theory, deliberate practice, and production-like scenarios.
3. **Mentorship design** — Structures that scale senior judgment without burning out experts.
4. **Institutional memory** — Searchable decision history, postmortems, and evolving glossaries.
