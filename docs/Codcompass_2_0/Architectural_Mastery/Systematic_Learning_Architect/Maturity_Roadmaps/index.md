# Maturity Roadmaps

## Core Theory & Mental Models

- **Maturity without vanity**: levels must predict operational outcomes—otherwise they gamify checkbox compliance.
- **Dimensionality**: reliability, security, delivery speed, and cost discipline mature on different curves—avoid single scores.
- **Pull vs. push adoption**: roadmaps succeed when teams see local benefit, not central mandate fatigue.
- **Sunset criteria**: advancing maturity sometimes requires retiring practices that once helped—document exits.

## Learning Path & Maturity Stages

1. **Baseline assessment** — Honest scoring with evidence, not aspirations.
2. **Prioritized runway** — Few concurrent initiatives with measurable deltas per quarter.
3. **Embedded coaching** — Architects partner on outcomes; assessments are developmental, not punitive.
4. **Continuous evolution** — Roadmaps versioned; criteria update when technology or regulation shifts the frontier.
