# References

## Core Theory & Mental Models

- **Canon vs. novelty**: prioritize timeless principles and peer-reviewed rigor over trend churn.
- **Layered reading**: foundational texts, practitioner syntheses, and standards documents serve different decision speeds.
- **Citation hygiene**: stable identifiers (DOI, standard numbers) outlast blog URLs.

## Learning Path & Maturity Stages

1. **Core bibliography** — Architecture, systems, operations, and ethics baselines agreed across leadership.
2. **Living bibliography** — Quarterly refresh with explicit rationale for additions and retirements.
3. **Synthesis practice** — Internal notes linking external references to Codcompass decisions—avoid orphan links.
